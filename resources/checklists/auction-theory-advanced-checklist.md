## üìã Pre-Trade Auction Analysis

### Volume Profile Setup
- [ ] **Identify POC (Point of Control)** - Price level with most volume (fair value)
- [ ] **Mark Value Area (VA)** - 70% of volume (acceptance zone)
- [ ] **Mark VAH (Value Area High)** - Top of value (resistance in uptrends)
- [ ] **Mark VAL (Value Area Low)** - Bottom of value (support in downtrends)
- [ ] **Identify HVN (High Volume Nodes)** - Support/resistance at high-volume areas
- [ ] **Identify LVN (Low Volume Nodes)** - Gaps = fast-move zones (breakout targets)

### Market Profile Analysis
- [ ] **Check profile shape** - P-shaped (trending), b-shaped (reversal), D-shaped (normal)
- [ ] **Identify initial balance (IB)** - First hour's range (reference for breakouts)
- [ ] **Look for single prints** - Gaps in TPO chart (unfilled value, potential magnets)
- [ ] **Check for poor highs/lows** - Single TPO at extremes (weak, likely to retrace)

### Auction Context
- [ ] **Determine auction type** - Rotational (ranging) vs. Directional (trending)
- [ ] **Check price location vs. value** - Above VA = premium, below VA = discount
- [ ] **Look for failed auctions** - Price tried to move but rejected back to value
- [ ] **Identify excess** - Price extremes with immediate sharp reversal (exhaustion)

### Trade Setup Identification
- [ ] **Value area retest setup** - Price leaves VA, returns for retest (buy low/sell high)
- [ ] **POC rejection setup** - Price hits POC and bounces (magnetic level)
- [ ] **Poor high/low fade** - Trade against weak extremes (single prints)
- [ ] **IB breakout setup** - Price breaks initial balance with volume (directional move)

## üí° Pro Tips

### Auction Theory Mastery
- **Combine with order flow** - Volume profile + footprint = institutional confirmation
- **Use multi-timeframe VA** - Daily VA provides macro context for intraday trades
- **Watch overnight inventory** - If price gaps outside value, expect return move
- **Monitor composite profiles** - Build multi-day profiles to see bigger picture value

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- ‚ùå Trading without checking where price is vs. value (context is everything)
- ‚ùå Fading strong directional auctions (don't fade trends, trade with them)
- ‚ùå Ignoring poor highs/lows (these are gift setups, don't miss them)
- ‚ùå Entering before acceptance (wait for price to stabilize at level)

### Auction-Based Strategy Selection
- **Rotational auction (ranging):** Trade value edges (buy VAL, sell VAH)
- **Directional auction (trending):** Trade breakouts from value, trail stops
- **Failed auction:** Fade the move, target return to POC/value
- **Excess at extremes:** Enter counter-trend immediately (exhaustion reversal)

### Volume Profile Quick Reference
- **Price above VA:** Premium area (sellers have edge, look for shorts)
- **Price below VA:** Discount area (buyers have edge, look for longs)
- **Price at POC:** Fair value (wait for directional break)
- **HVN zones:** Consolidation likely (support/resistance)
- **LVN zones:** Fast moves likely (targets for breakouts)
