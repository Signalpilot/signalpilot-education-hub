## ‚úÖ Objectivity Checks (All Must Be Met)

- [ ] **Echo Chamber Check**
  - Have I read OPPOSITE views today? (bulls if I'm bearish, bears if I'm bullish)
  - Am I following traders with opposing biases?
  - YES to both = ‚úÖ Good | NO to either = ‚ùå Echo chamber risk

- [ ] **Selective Memory Check**
  - Am I remembering wins more than losses from this pattern?
  - Have I tracked actual expectancy (not just "feeling")?
  - Tracked objectively = ‚úÖ Good | Just "feeling" = ‚ùå Bias risk

- [ ] **"This Time Is Different" Check**
  - Am I making an exception to my rules for this setup?
  - Am I telling myself "this one is special"?
  - NO exceptions = ‚úÖ Good | Making exceptions = ‚ùå Rationalization bias

- [ ] **Hindsight Bias Check**
  - Did I pre-document my thesis BEFORE the trade?
  - Or am I retrofitting the story after seeing the chart?
  - Pre-documented = ‚úÖ Good | Retrofitting = ‚ùå Hindsight bias

## üö´ Confirmation Bias Red Flags (Skip Trade if ANY Apply)

- [ ] Can't articulate the bear case (don't understand opposing view)
- [ ] Everyone in my feed/Discord agrees with me (echo chamber)
- [ ] Making exception to rules ("this setup is different")
- [ ] Can't refute even ONE bear case argument (thesis weak)
- [ ] Feeling 100% certain (overconfidence = blind spot)
