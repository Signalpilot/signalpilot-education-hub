Intermediate

Multi-TF alignment & HTF veto

Reading time ~10–12 min • Educational only

Three-chart stack

  • HTF (D/4H): bias & structure.
  • MTF (1H): participation & rhythm.
  • LTF (5–15m): timing.

Alignment rules

  • Only trade with HTF bias unless it’s a defined mean-reversion play.
  • HTF at supply/demand = shrink risk or veto longs/shorts that run into it.
  • Conflicts resolved by veto: when in doubt, no trade.

Workflow

  1. Scan HTF for trend, zones, anchors (AVWAP, opens).
  2. Check MTF participation (RVOL/OBV).
  3. Time entries on LTF with close-confirmed triggers.
