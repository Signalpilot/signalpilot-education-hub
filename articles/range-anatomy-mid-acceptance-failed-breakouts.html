Range anatomy: mid, acceptance, failed breakouts

Reading time ~10–12 min • Educational only

Mid & acceptance

Most ranges orbit a mid (value area). Acceptance = time spent. Moves away without RVOL often mean-revert to the mid.

Failure tells

  • Break above high on weak RVOL → quick close back inside → target mid or opposite edge.
  • Absorption wick at the edge + close back inside → treat as continuation of range.

Targets

  • Edge → mid (1–1.5R common)
  • Mid → opposite edge (needs RVOL and HTF alignment)

Checklist

  • Mark the mid; avoid buying the 5th test of the same edge.
  • Trade reclaims/BRR (break-retest-resume) with participation.
