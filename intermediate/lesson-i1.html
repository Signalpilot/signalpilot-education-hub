Multi‑Timeframe Analysis & Regimes

Regimes are the market’s weather patterns—trend, range, transition. Multi‑TF analysis lets you read the weather report before you pick a route.

Workflow

  • Define bias on higher TF (trend, key levels, volatility state).
  • Plan the trade on your execution TF; let lower TFs refine entries only.
  • When TFs disagree, trade smaller or stand aside.

Finding regimes

  • Trend signs: higher highs/lows, MA stacks, rising momentum.
  • Range signs: repeated rejections, failed breaks, compressing volatility.
Practice: For one symbol, label the last 30 days as Trend, Range, or Transition on the daily chart. Then check if your intraday trades aligned with those labels.
