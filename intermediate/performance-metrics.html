Performance Metrics: Beyond P&L

Intermediate18 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Use metrics that reflect quality.
  • Understand variance and streaks.
  • Report clearly.
Core Concepts
  • Key Metrics: Expectancy, Sharpe/SQN, Ulcer, Max DD, Win/Loss distribution.
  • Variance & Streaks: Path matters; same average, different journeys.
  • Reporting: Use R and %; avoid raw $ bragging.
Examples
  • High Sharpe with tiny capacity may still be weak.
Checklist
  • Graph equity and drawdowns monthly.
  • Report per setup.
Exercises
  1. Compute SQN and Ulcer for 100 trades.
  2. Plot distribution of R results.
Key Terms
TermDefinition
SQNSystem Quality Number.

Quick Quiz (self‑check)

Q1. Ulcer Index emphasizes:

  • A. Upside volatility
  • B. Downside drawdown duration/depth
  • C. Fees
  • D. Skew only

Answer: B
