Divergences & Confirmations

Intermediate18 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Define valid divergence rules.
  • Wait for confirmation triggers.
  • Avoid hindsight bias.
Core Concepts
  • Types: Momentum, volume, structure divergences.
  • Confirmations: Break of structure, close beyond level, signal confluence.
  • Invalidation: Where the idea is wrong.
Examples
  • Double divergence without break is still unconfirmed.
Checklist
  • Require confirmation beyond a line.
  • Avoid 'touch' entries.
Exercises
  1. Build a divergence checklist.
  2. Back‑review 30 cases.
Key Terms
TermDefinition
ConfirmationIndependent evidence supporting a setup.

Quick Quiz (self‑check)

Q1. Best practice:

  • A. Enter immediately
  • B. Wait for confirmation
  • C. Ignore always
  • D. Double size

Answer: B
