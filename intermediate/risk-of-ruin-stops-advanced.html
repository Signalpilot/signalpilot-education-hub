Risk of Ruin & Stop Placement (Advanced)

Intermediate22 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Estimate ruin risk from sizing/win rate.
  • Place stops with structure+volatility.
  • Avoid clustered stops.
Core Concepts
  • Ruin Intuition: Higher risk% → higher ruin probability.
  • Stop Logic: Structure beyond + ATR.
  • Distribution: Fat tails need wider safety margins.
Examples
  • Stops at obvious levels often get hit.
Checklist
  • Disperse stops; avoid round numbers.
Exercises
  1. Compute rough ruin risk for two levels.
  2. Test structure+ATR stops.
Key Terms
TermDefinition
Fat TailHigh probability of large moves.

Quick Quiz (self‑check)

Q1. Higher risk per trade generally:

  • A. Lowers ruin risk
  • B. Raises ruin risk
  • C. No effect
  • D. Guarantees profit

Answer: B
