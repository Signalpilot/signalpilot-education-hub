Expectancy, Drawdowns & Journaling

Expectancy turns outcomes into math: E = p × avgWin − (1−p) × avgLoss. A modest edge becomes powerful with disciplined sizing.

Drawdowns

  • Expect them. A 55% system can see long losing streaks.
  • Kill‑switch rules: reduce size or pause after XR or Y consecutive losses.

Journaling that works

  • Log: setup, regime, volatility, entry/exit, planned vs actual, slippage, emotion.
  • Weekly review: one leak to fix, one strength to scale.
Practice: Compute expectancy for your last 30 trades. Then simulate a 100‑trade path using that expectancy and variance to set realistic goals.
