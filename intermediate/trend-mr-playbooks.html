Trend vs Mean‑Reversion Playbooks

Intermediate20 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Differentiate trend vs MR setups.
  • Specify confirmation and invalidation.
  • Map exits by environment.
Core Concepts
  • Trend Playbook: Pullback entries with continuation signals.
  • MR Playbook: Fade extremes with strict confirms.
  • Exits: Structure, VWAP, R‑targets; trail logic.
Examples
  • MR fails hard in runaway trends.
Checklist
  • Identify trend day early; avoid fading it.
  • Use smaller size for MR by default.
Exercises
  1. Write rules for one trend and one MR setup.
  2. Define when MR is banned.
Key Terms
TermDefinition
Mean ReversionExpect price to revert toward an average.

Quick Quiz (self‑check)

Q1. MR risk:

  • A. Low variance
  • B. Runaway trend break
  • C. Guaranteed profits
  • D. Fixed spreads

Answer: B
