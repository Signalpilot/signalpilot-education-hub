VWAP & Anchored VWAP in Practice

Intermediate18 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Use VWAP as dynamic S/R.
  • Anchor VWAP to events.
  • Combine with structure.
Core Concepts
  • VWAP Basics: Volume‑weighted mean price.
  • Anchored VWAP: Start from a significant event.
  • Confluence: VWAP + level/zone improves odds.
Examples
  • Reclaims above VWAP often signal control by buyers.
Checklist
  • Avoid chasing far from VWAP.
  • Note VWAP flips post‑event.
Exercises
  1. Anchor VWAP to a recent event.
  2. Record two reclaim/reject cases.
