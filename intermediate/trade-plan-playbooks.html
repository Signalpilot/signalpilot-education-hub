Trade Plan & Playbooks: From Idea to Execution

Intermediate20 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Write an executable plan.
  • Use continuation/reversal playbooks.
  • Define no‑trade & kill switches.
Core Concepts
  • Plan Skeleton: Universe, risk, setups, execution, review.
  • Playbooks: Trend continuation vs reversal attempt.
  • No‑Trade: Major news, extreme illiquidity, fatigue.
  • Kill Switch: Max daily/weekly loss or errors.
Examples
  • Concise plans beat encyclopedias.
Checklist
  • Print playbooks; keep visible.
  • Stop after hitting daily loss cap.
Exercises
  1. Draft two one‑page playbooks.
  2. Define daily/weekly kill switches.
Key Terms
TermDefinition
Kill SwitchPredefined halt condition.

Quick Quiz (self‑check)

Q1. Plan should be:

  • A. Short and actionable
  • B. Long and theoretical
  • C. Memorized only
  • D. Broker default

Answer: A
