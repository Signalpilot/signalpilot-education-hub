Technical Edges: Categories & Evaluation

Intermediate22 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Identify indicator families.
  • Evaluate an edge without overfitting.
  • Separate filters from triggers.
Core Concepts
  • Families: Trend, momentum, volatility, volume/flow, levels/structure.
  • Edge: Positive expectancy after costs.
  • Filters vs Triggers: When to participate vs when to act.
  • Data‑Snooping: Parameter bloat fits noise.
Examples
  • Trend filter + expansion trigger is common & testable.
Checklist
  • Define filters and triggers per setup.
  • Keep parameters simple.
Exercises
  1. Write one setup with filter+trigger.
  2. List invalidation rules.
Key Terms
TermDefinition
ExpectancyAverage outcome per trade after costs.
TriggerEntry/exit condition.

Quick Quiz (self‑check)

Q1. Edge is:

  • A. Guaranteed profit
  • B. Positive expectancy after costs
  • C. High win rate only
  • D. Fancy math

Answer: B
