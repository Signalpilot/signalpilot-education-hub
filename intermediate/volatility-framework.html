Volatility Framework: ATR, Regimes & Squeezes

Intermediate20 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Classify regimes with ATR/stdev.
  • Adapt risk to regimes.
  • Read squeeze→expansion.
Core Concepts
  • ATR: Directional‑agnostic range measure.
  • StdDev/Bands: Compression vs expansion.
  • Regimes: Quiet/Normal/Wild; map playbooks.
  • Squeeze: Low stdev precedes break; direction needs confirmation.
Examples
  • ATR‑scaled stops adapt to conditions.
Checklist
  • Tag trades by regime in your journal.
  • Don’t assume squeeze direction.
Exercises
  1. Compute ATR and categorize 10 days.
  2. Mark one squeeze and break.
Key Terms
TermDefinition
ATRAverage True Range.

Quick Quiz (self‑check)

Q1. ATR ignores:

  • A. Direction
  • B. Range size
  • C. Gaps
  • D. True range

Answer: A
