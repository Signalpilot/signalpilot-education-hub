Position Sizing Methods (beyond basics)

Intermediate20 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Compare fixed‑fraction vs vol‑scaled.
  • Use caps and ladders.
  • Stabilize risk across assets.
Core Concepts
  • Fixed‑Fraction: Same % per trade.
  • Vol‑Scaled: Risk normalized by ATR or stdev.
  • Caps & Ladders: Max per position; pyramid rules.
Examples
  • Vol‑scaled helps equalize risk across symbols.
Checklist
  • Set caps by correlation and liquidity.
  • Ban martingale.
Exercises
  1. Simulate two sizing models.
  2. Pick one model to standardize.
Key Terms

Q1. Vol‑scaled aims to:

  • A. Increase variance
  • B. Equalize risk
  • C. Ignore volatility
  • D. Maximize trades
