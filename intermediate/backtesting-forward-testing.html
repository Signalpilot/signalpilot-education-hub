Backtesting & Forward Testing Without Fairy Dust

Intermediate24 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Avoid lookahead/repaint traps.
  • Model realistic costs.
  • Use walk‑forward & forward testing.
Core Concepts
  • Data Hygiene: Adjusted data, sessions, missing bars.
  • Lookahead: No future info; close‑confirmed signals only.
  • Costs: Spread, slippage, commissions; sensitivity tests.
  • Walk‑Forward: Optimize→test roll; mimic live adaptation.
  • Forward Test: Paper/micro‑live to validate ops & psychology.
Examples
  • Perfect top/bottom entries are a red flag.
Checklist
  • Record achievable vs theoretical P&L with costs.
  • Log parameter changes.
Exercises
  1. Design a 3m/1m WFA cycle.
  2. Add ±2× cost scenarios.
Key Terms
TermDefinition
Lookahead BiasUsing future info in a past decision.
