Market Structure & Levels: From S/R to Zones

Intermediate22 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Mark swings and BOS/CHOCH.
  • Use zones (SD/FVG/OB) as areas.
  • Blend with anchors like VWAP and session levels.
Core Concepts
  • Swings & BOS/CHOCH: Continuation vs character change.
  • Zones vs Lines: Auctions clear over areas.
  • Anchors: Session highs/lows, daily/weekly, VWAP.
Examples
  • Sweeps need confirmation; avoid guessing tops.
Checklist
  • Mark two HTF anchors first.
  • Treat first touch with respect.
Exercises
  1. Label BOS/CHOCH on a trend leg.
  2. Map daily H/L and VWAP reactions.
Key Terms
TermDefinition
VWAPVolume‑weighted average price.

Quick Quiz (self‑check)

Q1. Zones preferred because:

  • A. Prettier charts
  • B. Auctions clear over areas
  • C. Indicators require rectangles
  • D. Regulation

Answer: B
