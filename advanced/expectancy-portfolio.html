Expectancy, Sizing & Portfolio Construction

Advanced26 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Compute expectancy & interpret distributions.
  • Allocate risk across edges/instruments.
  • Manage correlation & concentration.
Core Concepts
  • Expectancy: E = Win%×AvgWin − Loss%×AvgLoss (R).
  • Sizing Models: Fixed‑fraction, vol‑scaled, capped pyramids.
  • Correlation: Hidden correlations spike in stress.
  • Risk Budget: Per‑edge and aggregate exposure caps.
Examples
  • Vol‑scaled keeps risk steadier across regimes.
Checklist
  • Limit per‑trade and aggregate risk.
  • Cut size when correlations spike.
Exercises
  1. Compute expectancy for sample inputs.
  2. Draft a 3‑asset risk budget.

Q1. Uncorrelated edges mainly:

  • A. Raise variance
  • B. Lower variance
  • C. Guarantee wins
  • D. Increase win rate
