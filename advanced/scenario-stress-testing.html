Scenario Analysis & Stress Testing

Advanced22 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Design historical & hypothetical scenarios.
  • Measure portfolio sensitivity.
  • Document outcomes & actions.
Core Concepts
  • Historical Scenarios: Replay known shocks.
  • Hypothetical: Custom shocks to factors/vol/liquidity.
  • Sensitivity: Change in P&L per unit change of a factor.
Examples
  • Stress uncovers hidden concentrations.
Checklist
  • Write actions for each scenario ahead of time.
Exercises
  1. Run two historical and one hypothetical stress.
  2. Summarize actions you’d take.
Key Terms
TermDefinition
SensitivityP&L change per unit move of a factor.

Quick Quiz (self‑check)

Q1. Stress testing helps:

  • A. Hide risk
  • B. Reveal vulnerabilities
  • C. Increase leverage
  • D. Reduce audit needs

Answer: B
