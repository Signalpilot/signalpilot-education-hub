Tail Risk & Crisis Playbook

Advanced22 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Prepare for extreme moves.
  • Define crisis protocols.
  • Recover methodically.
Core Concepts
  • Tail Events: Low probability, high impact.
  • Crisis Protocol: De‑risk quickly; tighten oversight.
  • Recovery: Gradual re‑risking; validate conditions.
Examples
  • Liquidity and correlations behave differently in tails.
Checklist
  • Focus on survival first, returns later.
  • Communicate plan if trading for others.
Exercises
  1. Write crisis triggers and actions.
  2. Document re‑risk checklist.
Key Terms
TermDefinition
Tail RiskRisk from extreme, rare outcomes.

Quick Quiz (self‑check)

Q1. During tails, correlations:

  • A. Fall
  • B. Are stable
  • C. Often jump
  • D. Reverse predictably

Answer: C
