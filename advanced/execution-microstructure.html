Execution & Microstructure: From Auction to Fill

Advanced24 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Understand auction mechanics & order book.
  • Select routes & order types by context.
  • Manage slippage in news/liquidity shifts.
Core Concepts
  • Auctions: Open/close concentrate liquidity; imbalance messages.
  • Maker‑Taker: Posting may earn rebate; taking pays.
  • Hidden/Iceberg: Displayed depth can understate true size.
  • News: Spreads widen; limits preferred or wait.
Examples
  • Posting can reduce fees but risks missing moves.
Checklist
  • Know your fee schedule.
  • Avoid thin minutes for size.
Exercises
  1. Compare posting vs taking outcomes.
  2. Write a news protocol.
Key Terms
TermDefinition

Q1. Maker‑taker:

  • B. Different for posting vs taking
  • C. Random
