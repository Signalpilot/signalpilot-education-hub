Walk‑Forward & Monte Carlo

Walk‑forward testing alternates training and unseen test windows. Monte Carlo resamples your equity curve to estimate the distribution of outcomes.

Why it matters

  • Edges drift. Parameters that worked in winter may degrade in summer.
  • Monte Carlo reveals plausible worst‑case drawdowns and time‑to‑recover.

Simple protocol

  • Split history into 4–6 rolling windows.
  • Tune on window N, trade on N+1 without touching knobs.
  • Aggregate OOS results; judge consistency more than the best period.
Practice: Take your last 200 trades. Shuffle and resample 1000 times to estimate 95% drawdown depth. Size so you can survive that scenario.
