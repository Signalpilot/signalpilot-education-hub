Portfolio & Correlation: Sizing Beyond One Chart

Diversification is not how many tickers you hold but how different they behave. Correlation can spike exactly when you need it low.

Principles

  • Volatility targeting: Allocate so each position contributes similar risk.
  • Correlation awareness: Reduce size when positions move together.
  • Heat cap: Maximum total risk across open trades (e.g., 3–5R).
Practice: Estimate daily correlation between your main symbols over 60 days. When it jumps, cut size or reduce the number of simultaneous trades.
