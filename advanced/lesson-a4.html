Regime Filters & Risk Control Playbook

Filters don’t find more trades—they prevent the dumb ones. Risk control rules keep a bad day from becoming a bad month.

Regime filters

  • Trade trend systems only when trend filters agree; rest in ranges.
  • Skip low‑liquidity sessions if your edge needs participation.
  • Pause during major news if your system isn’t built for event risk.

Risk control playbook

  • Daily loss limit (e.g., −2R), session cooldowns, and stop‑trading triggers.
  • “First loss best loss” rule when plan breaks.
  • Weekly review with a pre‑written recovery plan.
Practice: Write a one‑page “stress script” you’ll read when down −3R: what to stop doing, what to do next, and when to resume.
