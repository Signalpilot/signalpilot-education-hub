Futures Term Structure, Roll & Carry

Advanced24 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Read contango/backwardation.
  • Estimate roll yield impact.
  • Plan roll strategy.
Core Concepts
  • Term Structure: Prices across maturities; contango/backwardation.
  • Roll Yield: Gain/loss from rolling along curve.
  • Practical Rolls: Timing, liquidity, calendar spreads.
Examples
  • Carry can dominate long‑hold futures returns.
Checklist
  • Avoid holding illiquid contracts near expiry.
  • Check calendar spreads before rolling.
Exercises
  1. Classify a curve as contango/backwardation.
  2. Estimate roll yield for a simple curve.
Key Terms
TermDefinition
ContangoFar contracts more expensive than near.
BackwardationNear greater than far.

Quick Quiz (self‑check)

Q1. Roll yield is:

  • A. Commission only
  • B. P/L from moving along curve
  • C. Spread size
  • D. News effect

Answer: B
