Parameter Stability & Regime Overlays

Advanced24 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Test stability across regime changes.
  • Overlay regime filters without forcing flips.
  • Prefer plateaus over peaks.
Core Concepts
  • Stability Maps: Grid search to find broad plateaus.
  • Regime Overlay: Gate participation by volatility/trend regime.
  • FlipGuard Concept: Min‑hold + decay to avoid flip spam.
Examples
  • Edges living only at a single setting are fragile.
Checklist
  • Gate, don’t force; preserve diversity of edges.
Exercises
  1. Plot performance vs parameter heatmap.
  2. Gate by simple regime rule.
Key Terms
TermDefinition
PlateauWide region of good performance.

Quick Quiz (self‑check)

Q1. A sharp peak implies:

  • A. Robustness
  • B. Fragility risk
  • C. Infinite edge
  • D. No need to test

Answer: B
