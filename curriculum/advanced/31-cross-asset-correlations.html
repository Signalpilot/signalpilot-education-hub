Part 1: The Four Pillars (Your Macro Dashboard)

If You're Not Watching These, You're Trading Blind

Professional traders check these BEFORE every trade. Retail? They check Twitter instead.

Be different. Be informed.

üíµ US Dollar (DXY) - The Master Driver

When the dollar moves, EVERYTHING reacts.

DXY UP (Strong Dollar):
‚Üí Commodities DOWN (gold, oil priced in $)
‚Üí Foreign stocks DOWN (FX headwind)
‚Üí Crypto DOWN (risk-off, capital to USD)
‚Üí US exporters DOWN (goods expensive abroad)

DXY DOWN (Weak Dollar):
‚Üí Commodities UP (cheaper in USD terms)
‚Üí Foreign stocks UP (FX tailwind)
‚Üí Crypto UP (risk-on, alternative to USD)
‚Üí US exporters UP (competitive advantage)
Warning: If DXY is spiking while you're long crypto or gold, you're fighting the tide. Exit or reduce size IMMEDIATELY.

üìà Treasury Yields (TNX) - Growth Expectations

Bond yields show where smart money thinks rates are going.

Yields RISING (TNX up):
‚Üí Stocks DOWN (competing asset, discount rates up)
‚Üí Growth stocks HIT HARDEST (tech, ARKK)
‚Üí Value stocks OUTPERFORM (banks, energy)
‚Üí Gold DOWN (opportunity cost rises)

Yields FALLING (TNX down):
‚Üí Stocks UP (TINA: There Is No Alternative)
‚Üí Growth stocks OUTPERFORM (low rates = high P/E)
‚Üí Gold UP (safe haven + low opportunity cost)
Heads Up: When TNX breaks resistance (yields spike), expect SPY to correct 3-5% within days. It's not IF, it's WHEN.

üìä VIX (Volatility Index) - Fear Gauge

VIX measures market fear via SPX options.

VIX Level Market State Action
< 15 Complacency (risk-on) Trade normally
15-20 Normal Regular trading
20-30 Elevated fear Reduce size 50%
> 30 Panic Sit out OR buy dips
Real Talk: When VIX > 25, stop trying to be a hero. Cut size, widen stops, or just sit out. Your capital will thank you.

ü™ô Gold & Oil - Inflation & Growth

Gold = inflation hedge + safe haven. Oil = growth proxy.

GOLD Rising:
‚Üí Inflation fears OR risk-off (flight to safety)
‚Üí USD typically falling
‚Üí Real yields falling (10Y - inflation)

OIL Rising:
‚Üí Economic growth (demand) OR supply shock
‚Üí Inflation pressures (energy costs pass through)
‚Üí Energy stocks up, airlines/transport down
Pro Tip: When gold and oil BOTH spike simultaneously, it's usually inflation fears. Expect Fed hawkish talk ‚Üí stocks down.
Part 2: Key Correlations (The Relationships That Matter)

These Aren't Theories‚ÄîThey're Mechanical Relationships

When you see these divergences, pay attention. The market is screaming at you.

SPY vs TNX (Inverse Correlation)

Typical correlation: Negative (yields up = stocks down).

Scenario: TNX breaks resistance (4.0% ‚Üí 4.5%)

Why SPY drops:
- Higher yields = bonds more attractive vs. stocks
- Discount rate up = future earnings worth less
- Valuation compression (P/E multiples contract)

Expected Move:
TNX +0.5% ‚Üí SPY drops 3-5% within 3-7 days

Trade Setup:
IF TNX breaking resistance AND SPY hasn't reacted yet:
‚Üí Short SPY or reduce longs
‚Üí Target: 3-5% correction
‚Üí Stop: If TNX reverses back below potential breakout
Key Insight: TNX leads SPY by 1-3 days. When yields break out, don't wait‚ÄîSPY WILL follow.
DXY vs Gold (Strong Inverse)

Correlation: -0.7 to -0.9 (extremely inverse).

Why They're Inverse:
Gold priced in USD
Strong USD = gold more expensive globally
‚Üí Demand drops ‚Üí Gold falls

DXY rallies ‚Üí Gold falls
DXY drops ‚Üí Gold rallies

Trade Setup:
DXY breaks support (major level) ‚Üí Go long GLD
DXY breaks resistance ‚Üí Short GLD or avoid longs

Win Rate: 75%+ (one of most reliable correlations)
Crypto vs Nasdaq (High Correlation)

BTC/ETH correlate 0.6-0.8 with QQQ (Nasdaq).

Why They Move Together:
Crypto treated as risk-on tech asset (not yet uncorrelated)
Same buyers (retail + tech-focused institutions)

Leading Indicator:
Nasdaq often leads crypto by 6-24 hours

Trade Setup:
IF QQQ breaks down overnight (futures red):
‚Üí Expect BTC to follow within 6-24 hours
‚Üí Short BTC or exit longs before US open
Warning: When Nasdaq dumps 2%, expect crypto to dump 4-6% (2-3x leverage). Plan accordingly.
Part 3: Divergence Trading (High-Probability Warnings)

When Correlations Break, Pay Attention

Divergences are like smoke detectors. Something's about to break.

üìñ Case Study: SPY Up + VIX Up (Fear Rally)

The Setup:

SPY: $520 ‚Üí $525 (+1%)
VIX: 18 ‚Üí 22 (+22%, fear spiking)

Normal: SPY up = VIX down (inverse)
Actual: BOTH rising (divergence)

Interpretation:
Short covering (forced buying), not conviction
Rally driven by panic, not strength

Likely Outcome:
Rally fails within 1-2 sessions
Reversal back below $520

Trade:
Fade rally above $525
Short with tight stop above $526
Target: $520 (where rally started)

Result: SPY peaks at $525.80, reverses to $519.50 within 36 hours. Divergence traders profit 2.7R.

üí° The Aha Moment

When correlated assets move opposite directions, one of them is lying. The divergence WILL resolve. Trade the convergence, not the divergence.

Part 4: Pre-Market Intermarket Scan

The 5-Minute Macro Check (Before EVERY Trade)

Copy this checklist. Use it religiously. It'll save you thousands in avoided bad trades.

Pre-Market Checklist (Check These FIRST)

Asset Check This Implication
DXY Up/Down/Flat? Up = headwind for stocks/crypto
TNX Breaking resistance? Yes = bearish for growth stocks
VIX < 15, 15-20, > 20? > 20 = reduce size
Gold Spiking? Yes = safe haven demand (risk-off)
Oil Moving 2%+? Yes = inflation concerns

Confluence Matrix (Macro + Micro)

Before Taking SPY LONG:

Bullish Confirmations:
‚úì DXY falling or flat (weak dollar = tailwind)
‚úì TNX falling or stable (yields not spiking)
‚úì VIX < 20 (low fear)
‚úì Sector rotation into cyclicals (XLF, XLE up)

Bearish Warnings:
‚úó DXY spiking (strong dollar = headwind)
‚úó TNX breaking resistance (yield spike = bearish)
‚úó VIX > 20 and rising (fear increasing)
‚úó Sector rotation into defensives (XLU, XLP)

Decision Rule:
IF 3+ bullish confirmations ‚Üí High confidence long
IF 2+ bearish warnings ‚Üí Reduce size or skip

Example:
DXY flat, TNX stable, VIX 17, cyclicals up = 4 bullish ‚Üí GO
DXY +0.8%, TNX breaking out, VIX 23 = 3 bearish ‚Üí SKIP

üéì Key Takeaways

  • DXY up = commodities/crypto down: Inverse correlation -0.7 to -0.9
  • TNX up = stocks down: Especially growth (tech, ARKK). Leads SPY by 1-3 days
  • VIX > 20 = reduce size: > 30 = sit out entirely. Fear kills rallies
  • Divergences = warnings: SPY up + VIX up = unsustainable (short covering)
  • Pre-market scan = mandatory: 5-minute macro check before EVERY trade
  • Macro beats micro: Perfect setup + macro headwinds = losing trade
