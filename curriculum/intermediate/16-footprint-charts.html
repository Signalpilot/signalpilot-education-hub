Footprint Charts: Volume At Price Precision

Reading time ~16 min • Advanced Order Flow

Candlesticks show WHERE. Footprints show HOW. Two identical candles can have opposite implications—absorption (continuation) vs. exhaustion (reversal). Learn delta at price, imbalances, and integration with Signal Pilot.

What IS a Footprint Chart?

Breaks down each candle into buy/sell volume AT EACH PRICE LEVEL.

Traditional Candle:
Open: $100, High: $100.50, Low: $99.80, Close: $100.30
Volume: 10,000 shares

Footprint (same candle):
Price    Buy   Sell  Delta
$100.50  200   50    +150   ← Rejection (low vol at high)
$100.40  800   400   +400
$100.30  1500  1000  +500   ← Close
$100.20  2000  500   +1500  ← Strong buying
$100.10  1500  1800  -300   ← Selling pressure
$100.00  1000  2000  -1000  ← Heavy selling
$99.90   500   1500  -1000
$99.80   200   300   -100   ← Low tested

Now you see: Heavy selling at $100 (failed support), strong buying at $100.20 (absorption), weak buying at $100.50 (rejection).

Key Footprint Patterns

Pattern 1: Absorption (Reversal Setup)

Definition: High volume + high delta → Buyers/sellers absorbing all pressure.

Bullish Absorption:
$100.00  5000  1000  +4000  ← Massive buying

Interpretation: Strong buyer, absorbing all selling, reversal UP likely

Signal Pilot Integration: Janus sweep + footprint absorption = High probability long.

Pattern 2: Exhaustion (Fade Setup)

Definition: High volume + low/negative delta relative to direction → Weakness.

Bullish candle (+$0.50), but footprint shows:
$100.50  500   2000  -1500  ← Close near high, SELLING dominated

Interpretation: Despite bullish candle, sellers absorbed move
Result: Exhaustion → Reversal DOWN likely

Signal Pilot Integration: Plutus Flow spike + negative delta at top = Exhaustion confirmed.

Pattern 3: Imbalance (Momentum)

Definition: Extreme delta at specific price → One-sided flow.

$100.20  3000  100  +2900  ← 30:1 ratio

Thresholds:
- 3:1 = moderate
- 5:1 = strong
- 10:1+ = extreme (high probability continuation)

Pattern 4: Stacked Imbalances (Strong Momentum)

$100.30  2000  200  +1800  10:1
$100.20  3000  300  +2700  10:1
$100.10  2500  250  +2250  10:1

Interpretation: Aggressive buying every level, breakout likely

Reading Delta in Context

Positive Delta + Falling Price = Bearish

Candle: -$0.30 move (falling)
Total Delta: +5,000 (positive!)

Footprint:
$100.00  3000  1000  +2000  ← Low (heavy buying)
$100.30  1000  4000  -3000  ← High (heavy selling)

Interpretation: Selling dominated highs → Bearish

Key Question: "Where is the delta LOCATED?" (high, mid, low of range)

Practical Strategies

Strategy 1: Absorption Reversals

Setup:

  1. Janus sweep at $100 support
  2. Footprint shows absorption (+6,000 delta at $99.95-$100)
  3. Enter long $100.10, stop $99.85
  4. Target $101.50

Win Rate: 55-65%

Strategy 2: Exhaustion Fades

Setup:

  1. Rally to $101 resistance
  2. Footprint shows exhaustion (delta -3,000 despite bullish candle)
  3. Plutus Flow spike (climax)
  4. Enter short $100.90, stop $101.10

Win Rate: 50-60%

Strategy 3: Imbalance Breakouts

Setup:

  1. Breakout above $101
  2. 3+ consecutive imbalances (10:1+)
  3. Minimal Flow confirms trending
  4. Enter $101.25, stop $100.90

Win Rate: 60-70%

Complete Pre-Trade Checklist

Structural:

  • [ ] Janus Atlas sweep or breakout
  • [ ] Minimal Flow regime supports
  • [ ] HTF alignment

Footprint:

  • [ ] Absorption (reversal) OR Exhaustion (fade) OR Imbalance (breakout)
  • [ ] Delta placement supports thesis
  • [ ] Volume sufficient

Execution:

  • [ ] Entry defined
  • [ ] Stop beyond invalidation
  • [ ] Target > 2R

Key Takeaways

  • Footprints show HOW price moved
  • Absorption = strength (hold level)
  • Exhaustion = weakness (reject level)
  • Imbalances = momentum
  • Delta context > delta value
