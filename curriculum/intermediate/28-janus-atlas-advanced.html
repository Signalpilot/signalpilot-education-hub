Pattern #4: Failed Sweep (Trade Opposite)

Not all sweeps reverse. Some break through.

Here's how to spot the difference:

Failed Sweep Characteristics

What happens:

  1. Price sweeps $100 to $99.50
  2. Expected: Potential Reversal UP
  3. Reality: No reclaim within 5 candles
  4. Continues through to $98.50, $98.00 (potential breakdown)

Implication: Support was truly broken (not just swept).

Common approach: Consider short on continuation below swept level.

Trading Failed Sweeps

Setup:

  • Sweep below $100 to $99.50
  • NO reclaim within 5 candles
  • Continues to $98.50 (potential breakdown indicated)
  • Janus marks failed sweep

Your trade:

  • Example entry: $98.30 (potential breakdown indicated)
  • Example stop: $99.80 (above failed sweep)
  • Potential target: $95.00 (next support) = 2.2R

Expected performance: Moderate to high probability
