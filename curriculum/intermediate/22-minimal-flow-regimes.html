Minimal Flow: Regime-Based Trading Mastery

Reading time ~13 min • Signal Pilot Deep Dive

Same setup, different regime = opposite outcome. Minimal Flow detects trending, ranging, and volatile regimes. Learn to adapt your strategy to market conditions instead of fighting them.

The 3 Market Regimes

Regime 1: Trending (Directional)

Characteristics:

  • Clear higher highs/higher lows (uptrend) or lower lows/lower highs (downtrend)
  • Momentum indicators aligned
  • Volume on trend moves > volume on pullbacks

Strategy: Trade WITH trend (pullback entries, breakout continuations)

Avoid: Counter-trend, mean reversion

Regime 2: Ranging (Sideways)

Characteristics:

  • Price oscillating between defined support/resistance
  • No clear momentum
  • Volume balanced (no directional bias)

Strategy: Fade extremes (sell resistance, buy support)

Avoid: Breakout trading (high failure rate)

Regime 3: Volatile (Chaotic)

Characteristics:

  • Wide price swings, erratic movement
  • High ATR (3-5× normal)
  • News-driven or low liquidity

Strategy: Reduce size, widen stops, or sit out

Avoid: Normal position sizing, tight stops

How Minimal Flow Detects Regimes

Minimal Flow uses:

  • ADX (trend strength)
  • ATR (volatility)
  • Swing structure analysis
  • Volume patterns

Output: Clear regime classification (Trending Up/Down, Ranging, Volatile)

Regime-Specific Strategies

Trending Regime Strategy

Setup:

  1. Minimal Flow shows trending regime
  2. Price pulls back to HTF support (Daily EMA, Janus sweep zone)
  3. Plutus Flow POC aligns with pullback area
  4. Footprint shows absorption at pullback
  5. Enter WITH trend on reversal candle

Win Rate: 65-75%

Ranging Regime Strategy

Setup:

  1. Minimal Flow shows ranging regime
  2. Price at range extreme (support or resistance)
  3. Harmonic Oscillator shows extreme (5/5 overbought/oversold)
  4. Footprint shows exhaustion
  5. Enter fade (short at resistance, long at support)

Win Rate: 60-70% (but lower R, tight targets)

Volatile Regime Strategy

Action:

  • Reduce position size by 50%
  • Widen stops to 3× ATR
  • Only take A-grade setups
  • OR sit out entirely (best option)

Win Rate: Unpredictable (best to avoid)

Regime Shift Detection

Trending → Ranging

Signals:

  • Price making equal highs/lows (no longer trending)
  • Minimal Flow shifts to ranging
  • Volume declining

Action: Exit trend trades, switch to range-fade strategy

Ranging → Trending

Signals:

  • Breakout from range (HTF confirmation required)
  • Minimal Flow shifts to trending
  • Volume spike on breakout

Action: Enter WITH new trend, target extension

Any → Volatile

Signals:

  • ATR spikes 3-5×
  • News event, thin liquidity
  • Minimal Flow shifts to volatile

Action: Close positions or reduce size immediately

Complete Regime-Based Framework

Step 1: Identify Regime (Minimal Flow)

  • [ ] Trending Up/Down → Trade pullbacks WITH trend
  • [ ] Ranging → Fade extremes
  • [ ] Volatile → Reduce size or sit out

Step 2: Select Strategy

  • Trending: Janus sweep + absorption = long/short WITH trend
  • Ranging: Extremes + exhaustion = fade
  • Volatile: Only A-grade, reduced size

Step 3: Confirm with Other Indicators

  • Janus: Structure support
  • Plutus: Delta/POC alignment
  • Harmonic: Extreme readings (if ranging)

Key Takeaways

  • 3 regimes: Trending, Ranging, Volatile
  • Trending = trade WITH (pullbacks, breakouts)
  • Ranging = fade extremes
  • Volatile = reduce size or avoid
  • Regime shifts = strategy shifts
