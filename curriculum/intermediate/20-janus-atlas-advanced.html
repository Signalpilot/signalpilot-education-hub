Advanced Sweep Patterns

Pattern 1: Double Sweep (Highest Probability)

Definition: Price sweeps same level twice, then reverses.

First sweep: $100 → $99.50 → $100.50
Second sweep: $100 → $99.00 → $101.50 (stronger reversal)

Why: First sweep grabbed obvious stops. Second sweep grabbed traders who "bought the dip." Now all stops cleared → Reversal.

Win Rate: 70-80% (very high)

Pattern 2: Multi-Timeframe Sweep Confluence

Definition: Multiple timeframes have liquidity at same price.

Daily: Support at $100
4H: Support at $100
1H: Support at $100

Implication: MASSIVE liquidity cluster (sweeps from all timeframes converge)
Result: Sweep to $99.50 likely grabs $50M+ in stops

Win Rate after confluence sweep: 75-85%

Pattern 3: Sweep and Reclaim

Definition: Sweep below support, IMMEDIATE reclaim with volume.

12:00: Price at $100.50
12:05: Sweep to $99.40 (below $100 support)
12:06: IMMEDIATE reclaim to $100.20 (within 1-3 candles)
12:10: Volume spike + positive delta

Result: Strong reversal (failed breakdown)

Janus Integration: Janus marks sweep, then reclaim signal triggers entry.

Pattern 4: Failed Sweep (Trade Opposite)

Definition: Sweep occurs, but price continues through (doesn't reverse).

Price sweeps $100 to $99.50
Expected: Reversal to $101+
Reality: Continues to $98 (breakdown)

Implication: Support was truly broken (not just sweep)
Action: Enter SHORT on continuation

Janus Atlas Signals Decoded

Signal Types

  • Sweep Detected: Wick below/above structure, volume spike
  • Reclaim: Close back above (bullish) or below (bearish) swept level
  • Failed Sweep: No reclaim within 5 candles, continuation through
  • High Liquidity Zone: Multiple swing levels clustered (high probability target)

Complete Trading Frameworks

Framework 1: Classic Sweep Reversal

Setup:

  1. Janus identifies support at $100
  2. Price sweeps to $99.50
  3. Volume spike + rapid reclaim to $100.20
  4. Plutus Flow shows absorption (+6,000 delta)
  5. Minimal Flow confirms trending up regime

Entry: $100.30 (close above reclaim)

Stop: $99.30 (below sweep + ATR)

Target 1: $102 (previous high) = 5.7R

Target 2: $104 (HTF resistance) = 12.3R

Win Rate: 65-70%

Framework 2: Double Sweep Power Trade

Setup:

  1. First sweep: $100 → $99.50 → $100.50
  2. Price returns to $100
  3. Second sweep: $100 → $99.00 → $100.20
  4. Janus confirms double sweep
  5. Footprint shows massive absorption at $99-$99.50

Entry: $100.40 (aggressive reclaim)

Stop: $98.80 (below second sweep)

Target: $105 (HTF level) = 10R+

Win Rate: 70-80% (highest probability)

Framework 3: Failed Sweep Continuation

Setup:

  1. Sweep below $100 to $99.50
  2. NO reclaim within 5 candles
  3. Continues to $98.50 (breakdown)
  4. Janus marks failed sweep
  5. Enter SHORT on confirmation

Entry: $98.30 (breakdown confirmed)

Stop: $99.80 (above failed sweep)

Target: $95 (next support) = 2.2R

Win Rate: 60-65%

  • Only trade LTF sweeps aligned with HTF bias

MTF Janus (4H): Entry Zones

Combining Janus with Other Indicators

Janus + Plutus Flow

  • Sweep + Absorption: High probability reversal (positive delta at sweep)
  • Sweep + Exhaustion: Failed sweep likely (negative delta, continuation)

Janus + Minimal Flow

  • Sweep + Trending Regime: Trade pullback sweeps WITH trend
  • Sweep + Ranging Regime: Fade extremes (reversals at range edges)

Janus + Harmonic Oscillator

  • Sweep + Oversold (5/5 agreement): Extreme reversal probability
  • Sweep + Divergence: Additional confirmation

Common Mistakes

Mistake 1: Trading Every Sweep

Bad: "Janus showed sweep, entering immediately!"

Fix: Require reclaim + volume + order flow confirmation.

Mistake 2: Ignoring Failed Sweeps

Bad: Holding long after sweep failed to reclaim.

Fix: If no reclaim within 5 candles, exit. Consider reversal (short).

Mistake 3: Fighting HTF After Sweep

Bad: LTF sweep reversal long, but HTF downtrend.

Fix: Only trade sweeps aligned with HTF bias.
