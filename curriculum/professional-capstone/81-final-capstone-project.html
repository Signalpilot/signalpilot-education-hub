Final Capstone Project: Your Trading System Blueprint

You've learned 80 lessons. Now synthesize everything into YOUR complete trading system.

Capstone Requirements

Build a complete, documented trading system that includes ALL of the following components:

Part 1: Strategy Definition Document

  • Market & Instruments: What you trade (stocks, options, futures, crypto?)
  • Trading style: Scalping, day trading, swing, position?
  • Strategy types: Breakout, mean reversion, momentum (pick 1-2)
  • Setup criteria: Exact entry rules (e.g., "RSI < 30 + price > 200 MA + volume > 1.5× avg")
  • Entry trigger: Specific action that initiates trade
  • Stop-loss placement: Formula or rule (e.g., "2× ATR below entry")
  • Profit targets: Minimum R/R ratio, take-profit levels

Part 2: Risk Management Framework

  • Position sizing formula: How do you calculate shares to buy?
  • Per-trade risk: % of account risked (typically 0.5-1%)
  • Portfolio heat: Max total risk across all positions (typically 5%)
  • Daily loss limit: Max loss in single day before stopping (typically -2%)
  • Drawdown threshold: Max account decline before pausing (typically -15%)

Part 3: Backtesting Results

  • Test strategy on 2+ years historical data
  • Calculate: Win rate, avg win/loss, Sharpe ratio, max drawdown, profit factor
  • Out-of-sample validation (reserve 20-30% of data)
  • Walk-forward analysis (retrain quarterly, test next quarter)

Part 4: Execution Plan

  • Pre-market routine: What do you check before trading?
  • Intraday workflow: How do you scan for setups?
  • Order types: Market, limit, stop-loss automation?
  • Post-trade review: Journaling process, metrics to track

Part 5: Performance Tracking System

  • Trading journal (manual or software like TraderVue)
  • Weekly review checklist (metrics, rule violations, emotional patterns)
  • Monthly audit (strategy validation, regime check, expectancy calculation)

Deliverables (What You Create)

1. Trading Plan PDF (10-20 pages documenting all 5 parts above)

2. Backtest Report (charts, metrics, equity curve, drawdown analysis)

3. Paper Trading Log (minimum 20 trades following your exact rules)

4. Performance Dashboard (Excel/Google Sheets tracking key metrics)

Success Criteria

Your capstone project is complete when you can answer "YES" to ALL:

  • ✅ My strategy has positive expectancy over 100+ backtested trades
  • ✅ My risk management framework protects me from ruin (max 1% per trade)
  • ✅ I have documented, specific rules (no discretion allowed)
  • ✅ I paper-traded my system for 20+ trades (followed rules 95%+ of time)
  • ✅ My Sharpe ratio > 1.0 in backtest and paper trading
  • ✅ I can explain my edge in 2 sentences ("I profit because ___")

After Capstone: What's Next?

Phase 1: Extended paper trading (50-100 trades, 3-6 months)

Phase 2: Live trading (tiny size) (10-50 shares, validate execution)

Phase 3: Gradual scaling (increase size 25% every 3 months if profitable)

Never skip phases. Rushing destroys accounts. Patience compounds capital.

Capstone Project Components

  • 1. Strategy Definition: Markets, setups, entry/exit rules
  • 2. Risk Management: Position sizing, limits, thresholds
  • 3. Backtesting: 2+ years data, out-of-sample validation
  • 4. Execution Plan: Daily workflow, pre-market → post-trade
  • 5. Performance Tracking: Journal, weekly/monthly reviews
  • Success = positive expectancy + documented edge + disciplined execution
