Professional Risk Management Systems

Retail focuses on entries. Professionals focus on risk. This is why they survive and you don't.

The Professional Risk Stack (5 Layers)

Layer 1: Position-Level Risk (Per Trade)

  • 1% max risk per trade
  • Stop-loss placed immediately at entry
  • Position size = (Account × 1%) / (Entry - Stop)

Layer 2: Portfolio-Level Risk (Aggregate Heat)

  • Max 5% total risk across all positions (correlation-adjusted)
  • If adding new position pushes total risk > 5% → skip trade

Layer 3: Daily Loss Limit

  • Stop trading if down 2% in single day
  • Prevents revenge trading spiral

Layer 4: Drawdown Threshold

  • If account down 15% from peak → stop trading, review strategy
  • Either edge is broken or execution is broken (fix before continuing)

Layer 5: Black Swan Protection

  • Always keep 20-30% cash (never full leverage)
  • Buy far OTM puts quarterly (portfolio insurance, 0.5-1% cost)
  • Size down 50% during high-correlation regimes (diversification fails)

The Risk Dashboard (Track Daily)

Metric Formula Action Threshold
Portfolio Heat Σ position risks (correlation-adjusted) > 5% = stop adding positions
Daily P&L Today's gains/losses -2% = stop trading today
Drawdown (Peak - Current) / Peak > 15% = pause, review
Win Streak Consecutive winners > 5 = check for overconfidence
Loss Streak Consecutive losers > 3 = check for tilt/revenge trading

Professional Risk Principles

  • 5-layer risk system: Position → Portfolio → Daily → Drawdown → Black Swan
  • 1% per trade, 5% total portfolio heat (correlation-adjusted)
  • Daily loss limit = -2% (prevents revenge trading)
  • Drawdown threshold = 15% (pause and review strategy)
  • Always keep 20-30% cash (never full leverage)
