Crypto Market Microstructure: 24/7 Order Flow

Crypto markets never close. Funding rates, MEV, wash trading—everything is different.

CEX vs DEX

CEX: Order books, maker-taker fees, high leverage

DEX: AMMs, liquidity pools, MEV risk

Funding Rates

Positive funding: Longs pay shorts (overleveraged long → expect squeeze)

Negative funding: Shorts pay longs (overleveraged short → expect squeeze)

  • Funding rates reveal leverage extremes
  • MEV (front-running) exists on DEXs
  • Wash trading common — verify via spread
