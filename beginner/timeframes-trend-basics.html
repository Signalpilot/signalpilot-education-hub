Timeframes, Candles & Trend Basics

Beginner22 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Read candles without overfitting.
  • Define trends and handle multi‑timeframe conflicts.
  • Avoid timeframe shopping.
Core Concepts
  • Candles: Wicks show rejection; bodies show control.
  • Trend: Higher highs/lows up; lower highs/lows down; range = balance.
  • Multi‑TF: Choose a primary TF; use a guider TF.
  • Volatility & Range: Trend days expand; chop compresses.
Examples
  • Hammer after decline needs confirmation.
  • A 1‑minute downtrend can be a 1‑hour pullback.
Checklist
  • Pick a primary TF and a guider.
  • Trade with the primary trend.
Exercises
  1. Mark swing highs/lows and trend on a chart.
  2. Distinguish trend vs range day by range expansion.
