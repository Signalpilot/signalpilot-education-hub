Charts, Timeframes & Candles

Every candle is four facts: Open, High, Low, Close. Change the timeframe and you change the compression—and the story.

Timeframes

  • Lower TFs: More noise, more “opportunities,” higher costs.
  • Higher TFs: Smoother trends, fewer decisions, bigger stops.

Trend vs contradiction

A 5‑minute uptrend inside a daily downtrend is not a paradox; it’s a pullback on the higher TF. Choose the TF where your edge is designed to live and let others be context, not commands.

Candles worth knowing

  • Wide‑range candles: volatility expansion.
  • Inside bars: compression/squeeze.
  • Rejection wicks: failed push; interpret only with context.
Practice: Pick a symbol. Mark the last daily trend change. Then drop to 15‑min: annotate where that change began locally. This is the fractal nature of markets.
