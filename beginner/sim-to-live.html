Simulator to Live: Bridging the Gap

Beginner14 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Use paper trading to practice.
  • Transition with micro size.
  • Keep rules identical across modes.
Core Concepts
  • Paper First: Debug operations without risk.
  • Micro‑Live: Psychology changes; size tiny.
  • Rule Consistency: Don’t change setups between modes.
Examples
  • Small live losses teach slippage and emotions.
Checklist
  • Keep size ≤10% of planned live size at first.
  • Mirror exact rules.
Exercises
  1. Run two weeks paper then two weeks micro‑live.
  2. Compare mistake rates.
Key Terms
TermDefinition
Micro SizeTiny live exposure to test ops.

Quick Quiz (self‑check)

Q1. Main reason to go micro‑live:

  • A. Make fast money
  • B. Test ops & psychology
  • C. Prove indicators
  • D. Avoid taxes

Answer: B
