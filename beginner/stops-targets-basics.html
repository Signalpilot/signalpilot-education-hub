Stops & Targets Basics

Beginner18 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Place stops logically.
  • Define targets with structure or multiples.
  • Avoid moving stops without cause.
Core Concepts
  • Stop Logic: Beyond swing/level or ATR‑based.
  • Targets: R‑multiples, next level, partials.
  • Do/Don’t: Don’t widen stops; consider partial profits.
Examples
  • ATR stops adapt to regime better than fixed ticks.
Checklist
  • Write reasons to move a stop only tighter.
  • Set default 1.5–2.5R targets.
Exercises
  1. Backtest two stop methods on one setup.
  2. Compare drawdown and win rate.
Key Terms
TermDefinition
Partial ProfitTaking some off before final target.

Quick Quiz (self‑check)

Q1. Best reason to move a stop:

  • A. Hope
  • B. Twitter
  • C. Structure invalidation
  • D. Boredom

Answer: C
