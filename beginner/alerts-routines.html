Alerts & Daily Routine

Beginner12 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Use alerts to enforce patience.
  • Create opening and closing routines.
  • Reduce decision fatigue.
Core Concepts
  • Alerts: Act on close; reduce noise.
  • Open Routine: Calendar, levels, plan.
  • Close Routine: Journal, screenshots, review.
Examples
  • Routine beats improvisation over time.
Checklist
  • Keep 3–5 alerts per instrument.
  • Batch journaling after close.
Exercises
  1. Draft your open/close checklist.
  2. Implement alerts on one market.
Key Terms
TermDefinition
RoutineRepeatable steps that reduce randomness.

Quick Quiz (self‑check)

Q1. Alerts help by:

  • A. Creating FOMO
  • B. Reducing noise and enforcing rules
  • C. Guaranteeing fills
  • D. Replacing stops

Answer: B
