Charting Basics & Candlestick Literacy

Beginner18 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Read common candles and simple patterns.
  • Avoid treating single bars as guarantees.
  • Use candles within context.
Core Concepts
  • Bodies & Wicks: Momentum vs rejection.
  • Common Patterns: Engulfing, pin bars, inside bars.
  • Confirmation: Wait for follow‑through; location matters.
Examples
  • An inside bar after trend expansion often continues—if broken with volume.
Checklist
  • Never trade a single candle without context.
  • Note location: trend, level, volatility.
Exercises
  1. Collect 10 screenshots of valid/invalid pin bars.
  2. Mark context for each.
Key Terms
TermDefinition
EngulfingClose fully wraps prior body.
Inside BarRange within previous bar.

Quick Quiz (self‑check)

Q1. An inside bar means:

  • A. Guaranteed reversal
  • B. Contraction awaiting break
  • C. High volatility
  • D. No info

Answer: B
