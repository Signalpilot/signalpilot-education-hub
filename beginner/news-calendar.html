News & Economic Calendar Basics

Beginner16 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Know when major events occur.
  • Avoid trading size into unknown catalysts.
  • Differentiate scheduled vs unscheduled risk.
Core Concepts
  • Scheduled: CPI, rate decisions, earnings, jobs data.
  • Unscheduled: Company‑specific or surprise headlines.
  • Protocol: Reduce risk, widen stops, or stand aside.
Examples
  • Spreads often widen before CPI or earnings.
Checklist
  • Check calendar each morning.
  • Define rules for news windows.
Exercises
  1. Write a news protocol for your plan.
  2. List three events relevant to your market.
Key Terms
TermDefinition
Event RiskPrice impact from information releases.

Quick Quiz (self‑check)

Q1. Before big scheduled events:

  • A. Increase leverage
  • B. Ignore spreads
  • C. Consider standing aside or reducing risk
  • D. Move stops closer

Answer: C
