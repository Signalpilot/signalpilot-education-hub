Futures & Margin 101

Beginner20 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Understand contracts and tick values.
  • Know initial vs maintenance margin.
  • Handle roll and expiry basics.
Core Concepts
  • Contract Specs: Multiplier, tick size/value.
  • Margins: Initial vs maintenance; leverage implications.
  • Expiry & Roll: Move positions to next contract.
Examples
  • Maintenance breaches can auto‑liquidate.
Checklist
  • Know contract specs before trading.
  • Avoid holding through expiry unless planned.
Exercises
  1. Compute P/L per tick for a contract.
  2. Find next roll date for a contract.
Key Terms
TermDefinition
Tick ValueMonetary value per minimum price move.
RollShifting to a later contract.

Quick Quiz (self‑check)

Q1. Maintenance margin is:

  • A. Entry price
  • B. Min equity to keep position
  • C. Max leverage
  • D. Commission

Answer: B
