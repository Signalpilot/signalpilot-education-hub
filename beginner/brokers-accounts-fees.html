Beginner18 minGeneral education

Educational content, not financial advice.

Learning Objectives
  • Understand broker models and fees.
  • Know margin basics and risks.
  • Avoid hidden costs.
Core Concepts
  • Broker Models: ECN/STP vs internalization.
  • Fees: Commissions, spread, financing, data.
  • Margin: Leverage, maintenance margin, liquidation.
Examples
  • Low commissions can be offset by wide spreads.
  • Leverage cuts both ways.
Checklist
  • Read your broker fee schedule.
  • Keep leverage conservative.
Exercises

  2. Identify maintenance margin rules.
